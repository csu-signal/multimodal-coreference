Fire at Waitrose supermarket may have been arson
Police are continuing to investigate a fire at a Waitrose supermarket in Wellington .
Staff are today turning away customers .
Shoppers have been arriving at the supermarket unaware of the fire at the weekend .
Police say it may have been arson .
