Iran 's state television says a magnitude-5.2 quake has injured three people in the Persian Gulf Island of Qeshm .
The report says the earthquake struck the island at 5:11 p.m. local time .
The U.S. Geological Survey put the quake 's strength at magnitude-5.4 .
Monday 's earthquake was the second one to hit Qeshm in the past 24 hours .
Earlier on Sunday a 5.6 quake jolted the island , some 930 miles south of the capital , Tehran .
