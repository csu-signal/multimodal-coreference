Woman Killed In Queens Hit - And - Run Accident , Driver Charged
Updated 9 : 06 AM
A woman has died after being hit by a car that fled the scene in Queens , and now , the driver is facing manslaughter charges .
Witnesses say 59 - year - old Raj Chohan was leaving her car on 117th Street in South Richmond Hill Friday when she was hit by a silver Toyota Camry and sent flying into the air .
She died at the hospital .
Police caught up with the driver , who is from Schnectady , a few blocks away .
He has been charged with vehicular manslaughter , negligent homicide and fleeing the scene of an accident .
