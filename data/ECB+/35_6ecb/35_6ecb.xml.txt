Chargers ' Jackson arrested on suspicion of DUI
Chargers wide receiver Vincent Jackson was arrested early yesterday on suspicion of drunken driving .
Jackson failed sobriety tests shortly after 2 a.m. on state Route 52 in San Diego , said California Highway Patrol Officer Ray Scheidnes .
He said he did n't know Jackson 's blood alcohol level .
Jackson was on probation for a previous DUI arrest , Highway Patrol Officer Brad Baehr said .
He was taken to the San Diego County jail and released .
