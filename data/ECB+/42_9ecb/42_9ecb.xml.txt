Research in Motion today announced that T-Mobile USA will start selling the new BlackBerry Curve 8900 smartphone to its customers .
Dubbed as the thinnest and lightest QWERTY BlackBerry , the Curve 8900 will feature built-in GPS with support of location-based services , WiFi with voice and data support , a 3.2 megapixel camera with digital zoom , image stabilization and flash , video recording and playback , a music player , a 3.5mm stereo headset jack and a microSD/SDHC memory card slot with a 256MB card included in the box .
All this running on 512MHz processor .
T-Mobile is expected to launch the BlackBerry Curve 8900 smartphone in mid-February .
Price has not been revealed .
