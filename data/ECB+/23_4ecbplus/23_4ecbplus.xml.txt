Australian , English climbers fall to their deaths in New Zealand mountains
One Australian and one English climber have fallen to their deaths in New Zealand .
Police Inspector Dave Gaskin said Monday the two incidents on consecutive days in the Aoraki - Mt . Cook National Park were not related and were not due to bad weather .
He says they come as a reminder that climbers need to use extreme caution .
On Friday , 36 - year - old Duncan Raite died after slipping and falling about 60 metres ( 200 feet ) from a ridge .
