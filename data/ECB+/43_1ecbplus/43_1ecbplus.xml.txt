AMD Announces Strategic Investment to Accelerate Cloud Server Strategy

By acquiring SeaMicro , AMD is accelerating our disruptive server strategy , taking advantage of an inflection point in the industry driving opportunities in low power , client mobility , emerging markets and the cloud / megadata centers .
This disruptive move positions AMD to expand our current server offerings and provide our customers with low - power , low - cost , high - bandwidth platforms .
With SeaMicro , AMD builds upon our processor design and compute expertise with system - level advances in energy efficiency , total cost of ownership and density for customers .
