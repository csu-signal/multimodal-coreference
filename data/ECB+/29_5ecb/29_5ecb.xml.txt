Harrison out against Jaguars
Indianapolis Colts wide receiver Marvin Harrison did not play in Thursday night 's 31-24 win over Jacksonville due to a hamstring injury .
Harrison , who had n't missed a game all season , was listed as one of the inactive players .
He has 53 catches for 605 yards and five touchdowns .
He suffered the hamstring injury Sunday in a victory over Detroit .
