Chargers receiver arrested for DUI
San Diego Chargers receiver Vincent Jackson was arrested on suspicion of drunk driving on Tuesday morning , five days before a key NFL playoff game .
Police apprehended Jackson at about 2:30 a.m. and booked him for the misdemeanour before his release , making for a long night with a playoff looming on Sunday at Pittsburgh against the Steelers
Jackson enjoyed the best of his four NFL seasons in 2008 with 59 catches for 1,098 yards and seven touchdowns .
`` We 're aware of the off-the-field issue involving Vincent , '' Chargers general manager A.J. Butler said in a statement .
`` Obviously we 're disappointed .
We take these issues very seriously .
Moving forward , we will monitor the situation . ''
