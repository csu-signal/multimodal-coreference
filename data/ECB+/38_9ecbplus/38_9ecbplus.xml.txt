Magnitude 4 . 6 Earthquake Shakes Up North Bay
March 14 , 2013 5 : 22 AM
An earthquake with a preliminary magnitude of 4 . 6 was recorded in the North Bay Thursday morning , according to the U . S . Geological Survey .
The quake occurred at 2 : 09 a . m . about 14 miles north - northeast of Healdsburg and had a depth of 1 . 2 miles .
