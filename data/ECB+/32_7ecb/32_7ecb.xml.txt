An arraignment has been scheduled for Wednesday morning for a New Bedford , Massachusetts man accused of murdering two women .
Gary Gomes , 35 , will face murder and kidnapping charges after police found the bodies of two women -- 68 and 48-years-old -- inside an Independent Street apartment .
Neighbors had complained about a possible natural gas leak , which led them to the scene .
When police arrested Gomes on Tuesday night , he was barricaded inside the apartment with an unidentified person .
New Bedford Police said they will release more details about the investigation on Wednesday afternoon .
The identities of the victims has not yet been released .
