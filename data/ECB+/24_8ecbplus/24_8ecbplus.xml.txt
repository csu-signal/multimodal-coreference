Paris Store Hit by $2 . 6 Million Jewelry Heist
September 9 , 2013
Four men rammed their car into an upmarket jewelry store in central Paris on Monday , smashing the shop window and stealing $2 . 6 million ( two million Euros ) in jewelry , according to AFP .
The heist near Place Vendome is the latest to hit France after a string of high - profile robberies in Cannes .
A police source reported that the men drove a large four - by - four car into the store in the early hours of the morning , grabbing jewelry and luxury watches before setting fire to the vehicle and escaping in another car .
