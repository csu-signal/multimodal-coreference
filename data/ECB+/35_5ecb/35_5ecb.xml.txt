CHARGERS ' RECEIVER ARRESTED
San Diego Chargers wide receiver Vincent Jackson was arrested yesterday on suspicion of drunken driving .
Jackson failed sobriety tests shortly after 2 a.m. local time on state Route 52 , California Highway Patrol Officer Ray Scheidnes said .
Jackson was on probation for a previous similar arrest , he said .
Jackson , in a statement released through the NFL team , said he did n't think charges would be filed against him .
`` In the event they are , I look forward to clearing my name and proving my innocence , '' he said .
