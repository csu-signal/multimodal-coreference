'The Hunger Games' sequel is in negotiations .
04 / 04 / 2012
According to ''The Hollywood Reporter'' , Director of ''The Hunger Games'' , Gary Ross is currently in the negotiation stages to direct the sequel to ''The Hunger Games'' .
In terms of figures , ''The Hunger Games'' has already brought in over $363 million .
Apparently , ''Lionsgate'' would like a a November 2013 release date for ''The Hunger Games'' sequel .
