Plane bombs South Sudan refugee camp - witness
Thu , 10 Nov 2011 02 : 45 PM
A refugee camp was the target of an air strike in South Sudan's Unity state on Thursday , less than 50 km ( 30 miles ) from the tense border with Sudan , a Reuters witness said .
There appeared to be no casualties .
The Reuters correspondent heard a large explosion , then saw a crater about two metres ( 6 . 6 feet ) wide , an unexploded bomb wedged in the side of a school building and a white aircraft flying north from the Yida refugee camp .
Witnesses at the camp said there were three other explosions at 3 p . m . ( 1200 GMT ) .
