An overnight fire Friday at Alaska Gov. Sarah Palin 's home church caused an estimated $ 1 million in damage , and investigators say it could be the work of an arsonist .
Firefighters were called to Wasilla Bible Church in Wasilla , Alaska , about 9:40 p.m. Friday and found flames and smoke coming out windows at the back of the three-story structure , said James Steele , chief of the Central Mat-Su Fire Department .
