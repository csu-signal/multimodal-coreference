Sixers Hire Maurice Cheeks as Head Coach
Maurice Cheeks was officially introduced as the 21st head coach of the 76ers .
Cheeks , who played for the Sixers for 11 seasons and served as an assistant coach for seven , was named coach after Jim O'Brien was relieved of his coaching duties .
